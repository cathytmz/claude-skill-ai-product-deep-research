# Report Structure Reference

Use this as the default outline for Chinese AI product deep research reports.

## 1. 执行摘要

Summarize the product in 3-5 dense paragraphs:

- What the product is and who it serves.
- Why it matters in the current AI market.
- Its core competitive advantage.
- Its monetization/growth logic.
- Its most important risks and strategic outlook.

## 2. 公司背景与团队溯源

Cover:

- Legal entity, developer account, registration location, official website ownership.
- Founders, executives, advisors, team background, prior companies.
- Financing, investors, revenue self-sufficiency, acquisitions, strategic partnerships.
- Cross-border structure, brand positioning, and regulatory implications.

Use cautious language if only partial evidence exists.

## 3. 市场环境与行业概况

Cover:

- Product category and market size.
- Segment growth drivers.
- User behavior shifts.
- Regional differences in downloads, revenue, usage, willingness to pay.
- Why this product’s timing and positioning matter.

## 4. 产品功能深度剖析与技术架构解密

Cover:

- Core feature modules.
- Main user workflows.
- Feature/technology/user-value matrix.
- Likely AI pipeline and infrastructure.
- Signature feature case study.

Suggested feature matrix columns:

| 功能模块 | 技术实现推测 | 用户价值 | 典型场景 | 可信度 |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## 5. 用户体验与交互设计

Cover:

- Onboarding and activation.
- Core task flow.
- Prompting/editing/generation interaction.
- Paywall, ads, credits, waiting time, watermarks, sharing.
- Positive and negative user review patterns.
- Conversion-oriented patterns and possible dark patterns.

## 6. 商业模式与营收分析

Cover:

- Pricing tiers and billing cycles.
- Subscription, credits, one-time purchases, ads, B2B/API, marketplace, enterprise.
- Estimated revenue/downloads/ranking if sources exist.
- Unit economics signals: compute cost, CAC, retention, ARPU proxy.
- Refund, cancellation, customer support, and platform policy issues.

## 7. 竞争格局与竞品对比分析

Choose 3-5 competitors. Include both direct competitors and adjacent substitutes.

Then explain:

- Differentiation.
- Defensive moat.
- Weaknesses.
- Threat from platforms or foundation model providers.

## 8. 营销策略与增长路径

Cover:

- Content-led growth, SEO, community, virality, influencer/KOC, paid acquisition, affiliate, partnership.
- Product-led loops such as watermark, template sharing, invite credits, public galleries, generated assets.
- Viral case studies if available.
- How the product converts attention into revenue.

## 9. 风险评估与未来展望

Cover:

- Privacy and data security.
- Copyright and training data.
- Platform dependency.
- Model/API dependency.
- Abuse, safety, deepfake, misinformation, or domain-specific harms.
- Regulatory exposure.
- Product roadmap opportunities.

For each major risk, state the trigger and consequence.

## 10. 引用来源

List numbered sources with:

- Title
- Publisher or platform
- Publish date or access date
- URL

## 11. 信息可信度说明

End with a short note:

- High-confidence facts.
- Estimates and their limitations.
- Key inferences.
- Missing information that would improve the analysis.
